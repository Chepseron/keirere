What does the site offer?

Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industries and Fisheries provides farmers in the cluster with subsidized farm inputs, improved agricultural infrastructure, post-harvest handling technologies and competitive price for inputs and outputs. The e-Vouchers is intended to:

  • Place the choice of inputs back to the farmers rather than being given what they may not need;

  • Reduce incidences of late deliveries as the farmers will be at liberty to purchase/ pick from any of the registered input dealers;

  • Track use and application of right quality and quantity of agro-chemicals including the levels of productivity of the selected commodities;

  • Build the capacity of the private sector to respond to increasing demand for agro inputs.
